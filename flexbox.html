Green curry is thailandish dish.It contains green chillies and coconut cream.

Sisig is phillipino cuisine.made from parts of pig head and chicken liver, usually seasoned with calamansi, onions and chili peppers.

Lao Kaoh soi is a noodle dish eaten in Laos. It is a soup made with wide rice noodles, coarsely chopped pork, tomatoes, fermented soy beans, chillies, shallots, and garlic, then topped with pork rind, bean sprouts, chopped scallions, and chopped cilantro.

Pho

Pho is vietnamese noodle dish. Beef and chicken pho is often eaten by citizen, but chicken pho is more popular.

Tom yum kung is a type of hot and sour Thai soup, usually cooked with shrimp (prawn). Tom yum has its origin in Thailand.

Nasi goreng is an indonesian rice dish with pieces of meat and vegetables added.
